Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services do you offer?

Provide an overview of the specific IT services your company offers, such as software development, web development, IT consulting, cybersecurity, etc.

2. What industries do you specialize in?

Outline the industries your company has expertise in, whether it's healthcare, finance, e-commerce, etc.

3. Can you explain your development process?

Describe the methodology or processes your company follows in software or product development.

4. What technologies do you work with?

List the programming languages, frameworks, and technologies your company is proficient in.

5. How do you ensure the security of our data?

Explain the security measures your company has in place to protect client data and sensitive information.

6. How do you handle project management?

Explain your approach to project management, including communication channels, milestones, and client involvement.

7. What is your pricing model?

Clarify how your company structures its pricing—whether it's hourly rates, project-based, or a retainer model.

8. Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Explain your post-launch support and maintenance services, including any service-level agreements (SLAs).

9. Can you provide references or case studies?

Share success stories, case studies, or references from previous clients to build trust and showcase your company's capabilities.

10. What is your approach to handling changes in project scope?

Discuss how your company manages changes in project scope, including any associated costs or timeline adjustments.

11. How can we contact your support team?

Provide contact information and details on how clients can reach your support team for assistance.

12. What is your disaster recovery plan?

Briefly describe the measures in place to ensure business continuity and data recovery in the event of a disaster.

13. Are you open to collaboration or partnerships?

Indicate whether your company is open to collaboration, joint ventures, or partnerships with other businesses.
